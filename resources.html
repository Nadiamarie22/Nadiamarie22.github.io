Veteran Resources

We are actively building a verified network of trusted veteran resources. If you know a resource that should be listed here, please contact us. Our goal is to connect veterans and their families with support in mental health, housing, employment, legal assistance, healthcare, and community services.
  • Harvison House Helps veterans furnish their homes with basic household items and essentials.
  • The Mission Continues Volunteer and leadership opportunities for veterans looking to continue serving their communities.
  • Veteran Social Club A community-focused organization offering events, support, and connection for veterans.
  • Veterans Crisis Line 24/7 confidential support for veterans in crisis. Call or text 988, then press 1.
  • CareerOneStop Veterans Career tools, job resources, resume help, and employment support for veterans.
  • GI Bill / VA Education Benefits Education and training benefits for veterans, service members, and eligible family members.
  • Veterans Transition Resource Center Helps connect veterans with employment support, benefits guidance, and transition assistance.
  • Las Vegas Vet Center Counseling and support services for veterans and their families, including readjustment counseling.
  • VA Southern Nevada Healthcare System Local VA healthcare services for veterans in the Las Vegas area.
  • U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Access to healthcare, disability benefits, claims, and other federal veteran services.
  • WestCare Foundation Offers behavioral health, housing assistance, and treatment programs for veterans and others in need.
  • Nevada Department of Veterans Services State-level support for veterans including benefits guidance, housing programs, and service connections.
  • U.S.VETS Las Vegas Provides housing, employment assistance, and supportive services for veterans.
  • Nevada Legal Services Free or low-cost legal help for qualifying veterans and families in Nevada.
  • Veterans Justice Outreach Program Supports veterans involved in the justice system and connects them with treatment and services.
