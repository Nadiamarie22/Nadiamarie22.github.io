About Us

“For the people, by the people.” The Valor Bridge Foundation is committed to serving those who have served by providing comprehensive support to veterans at every stage of their journey—both those transitioning from military service to civilian life and those continuing to fight personal battles at home. Rooted in the principles of service, unity, and resilience, we deliver programs focused on community outreach, substance abuse and domestic violence support, trade skill development, employment assistance, resume building, and personalized guidance through the VA system, including disability claims navigation. We believe no veteran should stand alone. Through hands-on mentorship and advocacy, we act as a bridge—connecting veterans to the resources, opportunities, and support systems they deserve. Guided by the Hawaiian values of ʻOhana (family), respect, and community, we foster an inclusive environment where veterans and civilians come together as one. Through local events, outreach initiatives, and collaborative efforts, we aim to strengthen our communities, uplift our veterans, and ensure that help is always within reach. Together, we are not just transitioning—we are coming home as one.

Who We Are

We are a nonprofit foundation centered on service, dignity, and connection. We believe veterans should have easier access to programs, assistance, and community support without feeling lost in the process.

Our work is grounded in showing up, building trust, and creating a bridge between veterans and the help available to them.

What We Value

  • Service before self
  • Respect for every veteran’s journey
  • Strong community connection
  • Reliable outreach and support
  • Practical help that makes a difference
We also want the foundation’s identity to reflect Hawaiian culture through a sense of ohana, respect, and community care.
