Serving veterans through support, guidance, and community.

Valor Bridge Foundation is committed to helping veterans find programs, assistance, and real support through outreach, advocacy, and community connection.

Mission Statement

“For the people, by the people.”

The Valor Bridge Foundation is committed to serving those who have served by providing comprehensive support to veterans at every stage of their journey.

Rooted in service, unity, and resilience, we provide outreach, assistance, employment support, and guidance through the VA system.

Guided by Hawaiian values of ʻOhana, respect, and community, we bring veterans and civilians together as one.

Together, we are coming home as one.
